# 🎯 YouTube Family Privacy Hub
### A DPDP-Compliant Parental Consent Experience for Young Users

> Product Management Case Study & Interactive React Prototype built for the **Vedantu Product Management Internship**.

---

## 📖 Overview

India's **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023** introduces stricter requirements around collecting and processing children's personal data.

This project reimagines YouTube's onboarding and privacy experience for users under 18 by introducing a **privacy-first, parent-centric consent flow** that balances:

- 🛡️ Legal compliance
- ❤️ User trust
- 🚀 Product growth
- 🎯 Frictionless onboarding

Instead of treating consent as a one-time checkbox, this prototype introduces an ongoing relationship between parents, children, and the platform.

---

## 💡 Problem Statement

Today's onboarding experience relies heavily on self-declared age and provides limited visibility or control for parents.

This creates challenges around:

- Verifiable parental consent
- Transparency around data usage
- User trust
- DPDP compliance
- Children's privacy

The challenge was to redesign this experience while keeping onboarding simple enough that legitimate users are not discouraged.

---

# ✨ Solution

The proposed solution introduces a **Family Privacy Hub**, built around four key principles.

### 🛡️ Minimal Data Mode

Accounts identified as belonging to minors automatically start in a privacy-first mode until a parent approves additional permissions.

---

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Verifiable Parent Consent

Parents receive a secure notification, verify their identity using OTP, and review exactly how their child's data will be used.

---

### 📜 Trust Timeline *(Signature Feature)*

Instead of treating consent as a single event, every important privacy decision is recorded in a chronological timeline.

Parents can easily review:

- Initial consent
- Permission updates
- Data requests
- Consent changes
- Future milestones

making privacy transparent and auditable.

---

### 🎛️ Family Privacy Hub

A single place where parents can:

- Review permissions
- Modify consent
- Download data
- Request data deletion
- Understand why each permission is requested

# 🎯 Why the Trust Timeline?

Most products treat consent as something users complete once and forget.

The **Trust Timeline** transforms consent into an ongoing, transparent relationship by giving parents visibility into every privacy-related decision made throughout the lifecycle of their child's account.

This was introduced as the primary differentiating feature of the solution.

---

# ⚖️ Key Trade-offs

The solution intentionally accepts:

- Slightly longer onboarding
- Reduced ad personalization for unverified minors
- Additional engineering complexity

in exchange for:

- Greater user trust
- Better regulatory compliance
- Long-term platform credibility
- Safer experiences for young users
